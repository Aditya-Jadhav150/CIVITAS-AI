Civitas AI

3-Minute Demo Video Walkthrough Script

Target Duration 3:00 Minutes (180 Seconds)
Tone Professional, Urgent, Authoritative
Focus Gemini RAG, XGBoost ML, Actionable UI

1. Introduction & Telemetry Dashboard

0:00 - 0:45
Visuals (What to Show) Voiceover (What to Say)
• Show the homepage / Main Dashboard.
• Hover over the real-time telemetry metrics (Power Load, Traffic Flow, Water Quality, Emergency Wait times).
• Switch to Healthcare Intel and then Environment Prediction tabs briefly to show multi-domain analytics. 		"Welcome to Civitas AI, the next-generation decision intelligence engine designed for modern smart cities. During crisis events, urban administrators are flooded with raw data, yet lack actionable insights. Civitas AI solves this by fusing real-time sensor streams with predictive AI models.

Our central dashboard monitors critical systems—from power grids to emergency wait times. Specialized layers like Healthcare Intelligence and Environmental analytics provide unified, real-time situational awareness across the entire municipality."

2. GenAI Mission Control & RAG Triage

0:45 - 1:45
Visuals (What to Show) Voiceover (What to Say)
• Click on the Operations Workspace.
• Click "Initialize Operation" to spin up a new chat session.
• Type: "Heavy rainfall and waterlogging reported near Secunderabad station. Drainage is blocked." and press enter.
• Show the Gemini response generating dynamically.
• Zoom in/highlight the parsed Situation Summary, the Severity Badge (Critical), and the Sources Used (showing policies retrieved from ChromaDB). 		"Let's step into the Operations Workspace. During an active incident, operators can initialize a new operation instantly. I'll report localized flooding near the train station.

Behind the scenes, our RAG pipeline, built with LangChain and ChromaDB, retrieves the city's standard operating procedures and historical emergency records. It bundles this context and sends it to Google Gemini. Gemini acts as an AI commander, parsing the raw data into a structured JSON payload. The UI dynamically renders the situation severity, population impact, and specific policy documents used to back up its suggestions."

3. Predictive ML Scenario Simulator

1:45 - 2:40
Visuals (What to Show) Voiceover (What to Say)
• Click on Scenario Simulator in the sidebar.
• Drag the Rainfall slider to 180mm.
• Drag the River Level slider to 12m.
• Drag the Drainage Complaints slider to 85.
• Click "Run Simulation".
• Show the output panel generating results: Predicted Flood Risk (e.g. 84.8%), XAI Reasoning text, and the checklist under Automated Workflow Triggers. 		"Beyond reactive GenAI dispatch, Civitas AI is proactively predictive. Let's head to the Scenario Simulator. Here, operators can simulate potential weather disasters to stress-test city defenses.

When we increase rainfall, elevate the river level, and raise citizen drainage complaints, our pre-trained XGBoost model predicts a precise flood risk percentage. Rather than a black-box answer, our Explainable AI outlines exactly why the risk is elevated, and triggers automated workflows—such as recommending evacuation shelters and deploying mobile pumps."

💡 Hackathon Recording Tips:
  • Speak Clearly: Record the voiceover in a quiet room with a decent microphone. Speak with a confident, urgent but clear rhythm.
  • Match Action with Audio: Ensure your mouse movements, clicks, and typing sync up precisely with what you are describing in the audio.
  • Resolution & Zoom: Record at 1080p and slightly zoom in your browser (e.g., 110%) to make sure the metrics and text badges are readable in the video format.
